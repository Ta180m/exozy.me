Core

Start exploring our core services by signing up for an exozyme account! You'll also be able to use all the desktop apps and games installed on the server.


SSH: Log into exozyme from the command line


exodesk: Access your exozyme desktop from anywhere, powered by xrdp


exopages: Free static web hosting for all your websites


exocloud: Powerful collaboration and productivity suite, powered by Nextcloud


exochat: Chat with your friends on Matrix and other networks including Discord, powered by Synapse


exocode: Host your code on our Gitea


exostream: Stream your own media from our Jellyfin server


exocial: Join the best non-degenerate social media network, Mastodon


exotube: Watch peer-to-peer videos on our PeerTube


exowiki: Contribute to our wiki, powered by MediaWiki


exoffice: Real-time collaborative editing integrated with exocloud, powered by Collabora Online


Podman: Package and run applications in lightweight containers


QEMU/KVM: Quick and fast virtual machines
